Sonic the Hedgehog: Mario Edition is a SMBX episode which is a recreation of Sonic the Hedgehog from the Master System. This episode features six levels, each with a hidden Chaos Emerald to get you quicker to Bowser! You can play as all 5 classic SMBX characters, with Toad replaced with Sonic and his super speed! Please note that playing as an SMBX2 character or playing with two players may break the episode.
